Q: How to Access the Grievance Module in EPIS?
A: The Grievance module allows users to register and manage complaints raised by staff or the
public.
Steps:
i. Login in the EPIS system.
ii. On the homepage, click the Grievance icon or open the burger menu on the left side.
iii. Click the Grievance menu.
iv. Select the Transaction menu.
So, this will open the options related to grievance registration and management.

Q: How to search for an Audiology service in the system?
A: Users can search for a specific audiology test or service before recording the test results.
Steps:
i. Open the Audiology service page.
ii. Click the Service selection dropdown.
iii. Select the required audiology se rvice from the list.
iv. Alternatively, type the service name (for example, Pure Tone Audiometry) in the
search field.
v. Click the Show button to display the selected service.

Q: How do you register a deceased patient in the Forensic module?
A: To register a deceased patient:
i. Click on MLDI Registration.
ii. If the deceased has a CID number, enter the CID.
iii. Click the Get button to automatically fetch the personal details.
iv. If the deceased does not have any documents, you can enter the details manually.
v. Enter the following information:
• Name
• Sex
• Age
vi. In the Visit Details section, select the department as Department of For ensic and
Toxicology Medicine.
vii. Select the Doctor responsible for the case.
After entering the details, click save to complete the registration.
